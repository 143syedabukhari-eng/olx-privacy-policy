Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Data Extractor for OLX & Lead Scraper ("the Extension") handles user information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The Extension is a professional tool designed to help users extract marketplace listing data for lead generation and research. All data extraction and processing occur locally on your computer.

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information (names, emails, browsing history) to external servers.
  • Local Storage: Scraped data (titles, prices, phone numbers, specs) and your "Saved List" are stored 100% locally in your browser's internal storage.
  • User Control: You have full control. You can clear all stored data at any time by clicking the "Clear Data" button or removing items from your Saved list.

2. Permissions Justification

To provide a professional scraping experience, the extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Used to temporarily access the OLX page you are currently viewing to read listing content only when you click "Scrape".
  • storage: Essential for caching your scraped results and maintaining your "Saved List" so your work is not lost when you close the browser.
  • sidePanel: Enables the side-by-side interface, allowing you to browse OLX while managing your data in real-time without switching windows.
  • scripting: Required to execute the logic that unmasks verified contact numbers and handles "Load More" pagination on the page.
